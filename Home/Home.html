What is Citizenship Advancement Training (CAT)?

It is a mandatory secondary school program in the Philippines designed to foster social responsibility, leadership, and good citizenship by integrating military orientation, community service, and public safety components

Military Orientation

Students receive exposure to basic knowledge and activities related to military structure and discipline.

Community Service

Students participate in activities that contribute to their community and local development.

Public Safety

Educates students on public safety measures and how to assist in upholding law and order.
